About Heart Disease Risk Assessment

Mission

The mission is to reduce heart disease risk in communities by empowering Health Surveillance Assistants (HSAs) with digital tools for early detection, timely referrals, and better health surveillance.

Why It Matters

Heart disease is the leading global killer. The system helps to:

  • Detect risk early
  • Faster automated diagnosis
  • Clear health insights
  • Personalized risk assessment
  • Preventive guidance

Early intervention improves outcomes and quality of life.

How It Works

User Inputs

The healthcare provider or patient enters details such as age, blood pressure, cholesterol, family history, and lifestyle habits. This information provides the foundation for the risk assessment.

AI-Powered Analysis

The system applies advanced mathematical models to evaluate the information. It identifies patterns associated with heart disease and estimates the individual’s level of risk.

Risk Assessment

Based on the analysis, a personalized risk score is generated together with clear recommendations. This helps doctors and HSAs make informed decisions, encourage preventive action, and refer patients when necessary.
